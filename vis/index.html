From the word frequency analysis, all the frequent word are very simple and could be understood easily by all the publics. Most words only have one syllable or two syllables, and most words have the concrete meaning or not that abstract, which is easier to be understood and transferred.

From the sentimental analysis, we could see, as the first two speeches are delivered by a special occasion like the election victory speech or the nomination speech, they have more positive words. But towards to the normal speech delivered that not for a specific occasion, the word Trump used are more negation. On the one hand, as the republican candidate, he of course will speak a lot bad things that Democratic did before. On the other hand, he utilized the negative words to really show problems that existed or didn’t be addressed by the Democratic, and the picture he represented is more closed to the publics’ suffering. That’s the reason why people think he is more direct and brave to say anything without that much politician speaking styles, and that’ s the style he adopted to make all his speeches expressive and accessible to all the publics.

From the topics that got from the speech transcript, the most common word include american, country, nation, people, which are common in the president speech. Except in all those speech, the common topic mentioned in the speeches also includes immigration, job, law. If more speech transcripts added into the this text analysis, the topic analysis would be more informative to explore more topics under the each speech.
